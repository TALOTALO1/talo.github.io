About Me

Hello! I am Talha. Born in Turkey, my journey into technology started early at the age of 12. I was driven by a simple goal: to build my own digital world. Initially, I aimed to design and sell websites to local cafes. While that early venture didn't yield the commercial success I expected, it was the greatest classroom I could have asked for.

I learned that rejection isn't a failure—it's a lesson in persistence and growth. After those early trials, I turned inward. I spent countless nights coding, designing, and building my own web platforms from scratch. Even though financial success didn't come immediately, I gained something much more valuable: pure technical expertise and a unique design vision.

This path eventually led me to the frontier of technology: Artificial Intelligence. I asked myself, "Why just build a website when I can build a form of intelligence?" That question brought Walrus AI to life. Walrus is not just lines of code to me; it is the physical manifestation of my curiosity, my refusal to quit, and the dream of that 12-year-old boy.

Today, I am more than just a developer; I am a creator exploring how technology can touch human lives. My journey is proof that failure is just the first step toward innovation. Let’s build the future together.
